The Artist

Nedz is a London-based makeup and hair artist with a passion for bringing out each client's individual beauty. With experience across bridal, editorial, and commercial work, she brings precision, warmth, and artistry to every appointment.

Her approach is never one-size-fits-all. Every client is listened to, every look is considered from scratch. Whether she's preparing a bride on her wedding morning or working on set for a brand campaign, the same level of care and detail goes into every brushstroke.

Based in London, Nedz is available for work across the UK and for international travel bookings.
